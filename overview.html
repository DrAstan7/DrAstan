DR ASTAN

Advanced Shopper Tracking and Analysis Network

Introduction

DR ASTAN is an innovative system designed to track customer movements within retail stores and analyze shopper behavior in real time. By utilizing state-of-the-art AI and computer vision, we aim to provide retailers with actionable insights to enhance store layouts and boost sales.

Objective

Our goal is to automate the tracking of shopper behavior, capturing data such as foot traffic patterns and dwell times. DR ASTAN enables retailers to make data-driven decisions for an optimized shopping experience and higher customer satisfaction.

Methodology

Key technologies used in DR ASTAN:

  • YOLO (You Only Look Once) - real-time object detection
  • DeepSORT - continuous customer tracking
  • OpenCV - zone mapping and video processing
  • Pandas - data storage and processing
  • Power BI - data visualization and reporting
How It Works

DR ASTAN captures real-time video feeds, analyzing shopper movements and interactions within store zones. Data is processed to extract key metrics such as entry/exit times, dwell times, and customer flow patterns. The processed data is stored in CSV format and visualized through interactive dashboards in Power BI.

Core Features
  • Real-time customer movement tracking
  • Zone-based traffic analysis
  • Heatmaps and visual traffic metrics
  • Reporting on peak hours and high-interest areas
Future Enhancements
  • Detailed heatmap generation for deeper zone analysis
  • Time-based analysis to uncover trends
  • Real-time alerts for overcrowding and bottlenecks
  • Chatbot integration for rapid insights and responses
Conclusion

DR ASTAN revolutionizes retail customer behavior tracking by providing detailed, actionable insights through real-time data analysis. Future developments include advanced AI integration for predictive analytics and expanding its use to various industries, such as malls, smart cities, and security.
